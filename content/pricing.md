# Pricing

Our pricing depends on several factors — there's no one-size-fits-all rate. We'll work with you to find a package that fits your venue and budget.

## What affects the price

- **Group size** — larger groups may need extra answer sheets and a second host
- **Day of the week** — weekend and prime-time slots differ from weekday afternoons
- **Frequency** — one-off events cost more per night than a recurring weekly booking
- **Custom themes** — themed trivia nights (80s, sports, movies, etc.) involve extra prep

## What's always included

Every event comes with a professional host, laptop, answer sheets, and plug-and-play setup with your TV or projector — always a full two hours of live trivia.

Every venue is different. Tell us about your space, your crowd, and what you're looking for, and we'll put together a package that makes sense.
