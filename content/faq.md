# Frequently Asked Questions

Thinking about bringing trivia to your bar, pub, or private event? Here are answers to the questions we hear most often.

---

## What exactly does Binge Thinkers provide?

We bring a **professional host**, a full set of **trivia questions and rounds**, a **laptop** to run the game, and **answer sheets** for teams. Your host handles the pacing, the banter, and keeping the room engaged from start to finish. You bring the crowd — and ideally a screen.

## What does the venue need to provide?

At minimum, we need a **TV or projector** the host can plug into (HDMI), a space where people can gather in teams, and enough room for your expected crowd. That's it for equipment on your end. We also ask that you coordinate **prizes** with us — a bar tab, gift card, merch, or bragging rights all work. Food and drink service is on you, as usual.

## How long does a trivia night run?

A standard hosted night runs about **two hours** — enough time for several rounds, a break, and a final without wearing people out. If you need something shorter or longer for a private event, we can adjust. Just mention it when you reach out.

## How does booking work?

Fill out our [contact form](/contact) or give us a call. Tell us your preferred date, location, and roughly how many people you're expecting. We'll confirm availability within **24 hours** and put together a quote that fits your venue.

## Do we get to pick our host?

You don't need to — **we assign one** from our rotating team of experienced trivia hosts. Each one knows how to read a room, keep the energy up, and run a smooth night. If you've worked with us before and have a preference, mention it and we'll do our best.

## What kind of trivia do you run?

Our questions are written in-house and updated regularly. Think **pop culture, sports, history, music, and the kind of stuff people actually argue about at a bar** — not a school exam. Rounds are paced for a social crowd: competitive enough to matter, relaxed enough that everyone feels welcome.

## How is this different from running our own trivia?

We've been doing this for **over 15 years**, starting in South Korea and running nights across dozens of venues. Our hosts know how to handle a dead room, a loud room, and everything in between. You get a proven format, fresh questions every week, and someone whose only job that night is making your crowd have a good time.

## Can we book a one-off night, or do you do recurring trivia?

Both. We run **one-off private events** (corporate parties, fundraisers, special occasions) and **recurring weekly or monthly nights** at bars and pubs. Recurring nights are how most of our venues build a loyal regular crowd — we handle the consistency so you can focus on service.

## Do you do corporate or private events?

Yes. Corporate team events, holiday parties, brewery launches — if you have a group and a screen, we can run trivia for it. Private events can be customized: themed rounds, inside jokes, company-specific questions. Mention what you're planning on the [contact form](/contact) and we'll figure out the details.

## How many people can play?

Trivia works best with **teams of 3–6 people**, and we've run nights for crowds anywhere from 20 to 200+. There's no hard cap — tell us your expected headcount when you book and we'll scale the format accordingly.

## What about prizes?

We bring the questions; **prizes are coordinated between you and the host**. Most venues offer a bar tab, gift card, or round of drinks for the winning team. Some keep it simple with bragging rights. We'll talk through what makes sense for your crowd and budget before the night.

## How much does it cost?

Every venue is different — group size, day of the week, frequency, and whether you need something custom all affect pricing. We don't publish fixed rates. **[Ask us for a quote](/contact)** and we'll put together something that fits your space and your crowd.

## How far in advance should we book?

For a **one-off event**, a few weeks is usually enough. For a **recurring night** or a popular date (holiday weekends, etc.), earlier is better. Reach out as soon as you have a date in mind — we'll let you know what's available.

## What areas do you serve?

We're based in **Winnipeg** and building our venue roster here. If you're outside the city or planning something special, get in touch anyway — we'll let you know what's possible.
