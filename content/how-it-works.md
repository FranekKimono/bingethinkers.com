# How It Works

Booking a trivia night with Binge Thinkers is simple. Here's how it goes down.

## 1. Reach Out

Fill out our [contact form](/contact) or give us a call. Tell us your preferred date, location, and how many people you're expecting. We'll confirm availability within 24 hours.

## 2. We Assign a Host

We match you with one of our rotating hosts — experienced trivia masters who know how to read a room and keep the energy going. No need to pick; we handle it.

## 3. Host Arrives, Trivia Begins

On the night, your host shows up with a laptop and answer sheets. They plug into your TV or projector, run through rounds of trivia, and keep the crowd engaged for a full two hours. You sit back and enjoy.

## 4. Prizes Are on You (and Us)

We bring the questions. You and the host coordinate prizes — whether it's a bar tab, merch, or bragging rights.
